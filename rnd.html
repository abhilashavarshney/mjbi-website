Our Vision


Our Research and Development (R&D) meets the challenges farmers face by providing them with crop protection and seeds innovations that are sustainable and safe for people and the environment. Our research is designed to improve the way crops are grown and protected so that everyone, from consumers, to farmers, to the environment, benefits. For our innovations to benefit all, they must contribute to the sustainability of our planet through helping to address a complex, interconnected set of global challenges. They need to provide safe, healthy and nutritious food, which meets consumersâ€™ changing expectations and tastes. They need to help farmers adapt to extreme weather caused by climate change, as well as mitigate the carbon emissions from agriculture. They also need to support farmers in counteracting soil erosion and biodiversity loss.
